Ravi Sharma

Tradition: Madhubani Painting

Region: Bihar, India

About the Artist

Ravi Sharma is a third-generation Madhubani artist whose works reflect the deep cultural heritage of Bihar. He learned the intricate techniques from his grandmother and has carried forward the tradition with contemporary touches that resonate with global audiences. Ravi’s journey is not only about preserving art but also about sharing stories of resilience, identity, and creativity.

The Story Behind the Art

Each of Ravi’s paintings is inspired by folk tales, mythology, and the environment around him. His art often uses natural dyes and handmade paper, keeping the craft sustainable. His recent works focus on themes of climate change and harmony between nature and humans.
